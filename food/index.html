Eating in the Mission

This summer, I'm interning at Picnic Health with Roy Fu and we've decided to take on all of the Mission District's gastronomical delights. Below is a list of restaurants, hole-in-the-walls, and food trucks and our experiences with them! List inspired by 7x7's 20 Best Dishes in the Mission.

The List

  • Pal's Takeaway

    This place is really hidden away! -- it's located in a small little alcohol store and not something you'd walk by on the street. The owner is suprisingly nice and was really quick with the sandwiches - he offered to cater our office and give us a discount if we returned! Alas we didn't. They do change their sandwiches every week so it might worth visiting again.
